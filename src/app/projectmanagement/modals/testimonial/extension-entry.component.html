As an academic cloud providing free resources, we rely on our users' support.

To ensure the continued availability of these services, we kindly ask for your help in two ways:

  • Publication acknowledgement: If you've published research resulting from work done in the de.NBI Cloud or related to it, please enter the DOIs of those publications below. This helps us demonstrate the impact of our services and secure future funding.
  • User testimonials: We'd love to hear about your positive experiences with the cloud! Your testimonial will help us promote the service to other researchers and make it more widely available. Please use the form on this project overview page to share your story. You can find already published testimonials here.

We really appreciate your help!
